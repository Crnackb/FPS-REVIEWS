Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III

PC / PS5 / Xbox 2023 Reviewed by Your Name Here
9.2 / 10 Excellent

A tightly-tuned multiplayer loop carries a forgettable campaign — the gunplay is the best the series has felt in years, and it's the reason to buy in.

Pros

  • Best-in-class gunfeel and weapon customization
  • Maps built specifically for competitive play
  • Strong netcode, minimal desync in ranked play

Cons

  • Campaign is short and narratively thin
  • Monetization is aggressive in the store
  • Some legacy maps feel undercooked in this engine

Gameplay

The moment-to-moment gunplay is the standout here. Recoil patterns feel readable, time-to-kill is consistent, and the movement tech (slide-canceling, tac-sprint) rewards players who put in the hours without locking out newcomers entirely.

Graphics & Performance

Running on the updated engine, lighting and destructible environments are a clear step up from the previous entry. On PC, frame pacing stayed stable even during full 20v20 Ground War matches.

Multiplayer

This is where the game earns its score. Map design leans back toward the three-lane structure the community has asked for, and ranked play finally feels like it rewards positioning over pure reaction time.

Final Verdict

If multiplayer is why you buy a Call of Duty game, this is an easy recommendation. Skip it if you're only here for the campaign.
